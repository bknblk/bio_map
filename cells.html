Cells

Cells are the building block behind all of life. All cells, regardless of domain, contain cells. All cells contain Plasma Membrace, a place for DNA, ribosomes, and cytoplasm. Some key points of cells, known as cell theory is that:

  1. All organisms are composed of one or more cells, and the life processes of metabolism and heredity occur within these cells
  2. Cells are the smallest living things, the bnasic units or orgnaization of all organisms
  3. Cells arise only be division of a previously existing cell
